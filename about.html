What is this?

The Carbon Calculator estimates your personal weekly CO₂ footprint from four categories of consumer behavior — diet, shipping, clothing, and flights.

Results are placed in context against real per-capita emissions data from 17 countries, visualised as an interactive bubble chart.

Who made this?

How are emissions calculated?

Emissions are estimated using the Climatiq API, which applies peer-reviewed emission factors to your inputs.

  • Diet — weekly meat consumption weight and type.
  • Packages — van freight scaled by packages per week.
  • Clothing — monthly spend on new garments; secondhand discounted 80%.
  • Flights — per flight using actual route distance, amortised weekly.

Where does the country data come from?

Per-capita figures for flights, meat, clothing, and e-commerce CO₂ are drawn from publicly available national statistics and consumption research datasets.

All values represent approximate annual averages per person, converted to a common unit for comparison.

Emission estimates are approximations for educational purposes and should not be used for carbon accounting or compliance reporting.
